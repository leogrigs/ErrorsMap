How does this application work?

This is an angular application integrated with a ContentStack platform. To create a guide or report a error, first:

- Go to Guides & Error Mapping Stack on ContentStack

- In the left side, go to Entries

- Click on "+ New Entry" and then choose what you want (Guide or Error).

- Complete the infos and everything is ready! Your entry will appear soon in this application!



OBS: You don't need to write a step-by-step on the first moment, your entry could be filled in another moment by another one!
